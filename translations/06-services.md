## Reviewer notes for services

- **"Maatskappysekretarisdienste"** — official Afrikaans for "Company Secretary Services". Long compound but standard.
- **"Posevaluering"** for "JobEvaluation" — standard term in SA HR.
- **"Bedryfsverhoudinge"** for "Industrial Relations" — official statutory term.
- **"Werkplek-welstandsprogramme"** uses hyphen per Afrikaans compound rules.
- **"GI"** is the Afrikaans abbreviation for "Gelyke Indiensneming" (Employment Equity / EE).
- **"BV"** is the Afrikaans abbreviation for "Bedryfsverhoudinge" (Industrial Relations / IR).
- **"DvA"** is the Afrikaans abbreviation for "Departement van Arbeid" (DOL).
- Each service intro keeps the Afrikaans warm and direct rather than over-formal — matches the English brand voice.
- Service detail deep content (whyOptiHR, legal considerations, FAQ, sections) is in `06b-service-details.md`.
