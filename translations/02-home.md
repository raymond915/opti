## Reviewer notes for home page

- **"Bestuur jou onderneming"** — translated literally "Run your business". Could also be "Hou jou onderneming aan die gang" if more idiomatic preferred.
- **"vervolmaak"** for "perfect" — formal. "Perfekteer" is also acceptable but feels imported.
- The body copy intentionally keeps OptiAI in English (brand name).
- The compound noun "MH-nakoming" follows Afrikaans hyphenation convention for acronym + noun.
- Acronyms (CCMA, BCEA, LRA, EEA) — first mention spells out in Afrikaans then keeps acronym; subsequent mentions use just the acronym.
