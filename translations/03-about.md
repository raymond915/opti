## Reviewer notes for about page

- **"toegelate prokureur"** — official Afrikaans for "admitted attorney" (legally precise term).
- **"verskyningsreg"** — "right of appearance" is a precise legal term in Afrikaans.
- **"Suksessyfer van 95%+"** — using the percent symbol is fine in Afrikaans business writing.
- **Card 6 "Integrated HR & Legal Under One Roof"** — "onder Een Dak" preserves the idiom; alternative would be "in een aanbieding".
- **"vakbond-geskil"** uses hyphen per Afrikaans compound rules.
