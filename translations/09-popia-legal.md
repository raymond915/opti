## Reviewer notes for POPIA & Legal pages

- **"Inligtingsreguleerder"** for "Information Regulator" — official Afrikaans term used by the SA government.
- **"Edms Bpk"** = "Eiendoms Beperk" for "(Pty) Ltd" — standard SA business form.
- **"WBD"** = "Wet op Basiese Diensvoorwaardes" (BCEA), introduced earlier.
- **"WAV"** = "Wet op Arbeidsverhoudinge" (LRA), introduced earlier.
- **"WEKT"** = "Wet op Elektroniese Kommunikasie en Transaksies" (ECTA) — official.
- **"SAID"** = "Suid-Afrikaanse Inkomstediens" (SARS) — official.
- **"Operateur"** for "operator" in POPIA section 2.3 — official POPIA term in SA Afrikaans law text.
- **"Verantwoordelike party"** for "responsible party" — official POPIA term.
- **"Data-subjek"** for "data subject" — official POPIA term.
- **"Persoonlike inligting"** for "personal information" — official POPIA term (NOT "persoonlike data").
- **Email addresses, URLs, phone numbers** kept identical.
- **"Privaatheidskennis"** for "Privacy Notice" — formal legal term; alternative "Privaatheidsverklaring" also acceptable.
- **"Koekie"** is the standard direct translation; "Cookie" untranslated is also widely used in Afrikaans tech writing.
- ⚠️ Final legal text MUST be reviewed by an Afrikaans-speaking attorney before publication.
