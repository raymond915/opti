## Reviewer notes for insights

- **"OptiBuzz"** kept English — brand name.
- **"WGI"** = "Wet op Gelyke Indiensneming" (Employment Equity Act / EEA).
- **"WAV"** = "Wet op Arbeidsverhoudinge" (LRA).
- **"WBD"** = "Wet op Basiese Diensvoorwaardes" (BCEA).
- **"KVBA"** = "Kommissie vir Versoening, Bemiddeling en Arbitrasie" (CCMA).
- **"Versoening"** for "conciliation" — legal SA term.
- **"Arbitrasie"** for "arbitration" — direct cognate.
- **"Posevaluering"** for "job evaluation" — standard SA HR.
- **"Diensskontrak"** for "employment contract" — formal term.
- **Patterson** kept English — international job grading system.
- **Article body content** is 16 separate articles × 5–8 sections each. Recommend authoring fresh in Afrikaans (rather than translating) so the writing feels native.
- **Date formatting:** Convert "3 March 2026" → "3 Maart 2026" by mapping month names per the table above.
- **"min read"** → "min lees" — common in SA Afrikaans web copy.
